    Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore

    VIT Vellore founded in the year 1984 as Vellore Engineering College and was established with the aim to provide global standard higher education to students in fields such as Science, Engineering & Technology, Business Administration, Design, Law. It persistently seeks to adopt innovative methods to improve the quality of higher education in India on a consistent basis. VIT Vellore is affiliated with the VIT University and is accredited from UGC and NAAC.

    CASCADE 2022

    CASCADE is a hackathon conducted by the IEEE CAS student chapter of VIT Vellore. It was first hosted in February, 2022, and it aims to provide students with a new platform for innovation, connectivity and knowledge. It is complete with 5 different tracks, namely, Circuitry, Fintech, Health Care, Education, and of course, Open Innovation.

    Track 5

    Open Innovation

    In a time where unique solutions are required not only in the domain of technology but others as well, open innovation provides an incentive to students and thinkers to come forth and present problems plaguing their field and come up with a solution. This is an open platform where students and innovators from across the country are welcome to come up with their own problem statement in any software or hardware related domain and provide a solution for the same. The purpose of open innovation is to push students to their limits, encourage them to take risks and generate ideas which can be worked upon for future use.
